Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a strategic analysis of human capital, focusing on the intersection of employee performance, retention trends, and financial implications. The goal is to empower HR leaders with data-driven insights to reduce turnover and optimize compensation strategies.

Key Metrics & Dashboards
The project consists of two main analytical views:

1. Workforce Overview 


Total Employees: Managed a dataset of 1,470 employees.


Attrition Analysis: Tracked a 16.12% attrition rate.


Employee Sentiment: Average Manager Rating of 3.5 and a Work-Life Balance score of 3.41.


Demographics: Detailed breakdown of Gender Distribution (45.9% Female, 44.3% Male) and Education Levels.

2. Performance & Finance 

Financial Impact: Calculated an Estimated Attrition Cost of $29M.

Compensation Efficiency: Average Salary Active sits at $118.9K with a Salary Efficiency Index of 0.03.

Performance Matrix: A filtered analysis of the Top 5 Job Roles. For example, Data Scientists achieved an average rating of 3.53 with an average salary of $57,632.

Technical Implementation

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Developed custom measures for Estimated Attrition Cost, Workforce Wellbeing Gap, and Salary Efficiency Index.


Data Visualization: Implemented Ribbon Charts for trend analysis and Sankey-style flows for wellbeing tracking.

UI/UX: Applied a professional Petroleum Blue & Teal color palette to enhance scannability and decision-making.
